## Interpretation

The Android product wrapper is implemented and compiles into an APK in hosted
CI. The iOS product wrapper compiles into a no-sign simulator app. Neither
result proves installation, launch, WebView behavior, OS keychain behavior,
background audio lifecycle, or resource release on a physical device.

The historical Android iframe failure remains a real failed experiment. The
product response is fail-closed non-enablement of imported JavaScript and Lua,
not a claim that stock same-process Tauri WebViews became an isolation or
termination boundary.
